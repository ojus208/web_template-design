Frequently Asked Question

Who are eligible for the training?

You need no prior knowledge for this training. You will learn everything from scratch. Targeted towards college students who are willing to scale up or hone their skills.

When and where will the training be given?

The training is completely online and you would be given access to the training from your Internshipgate dashboard. The project problem statement and tests will be inside the training. You will also be awarded a certificate for the training post completion.

What is the duration of the training program?

You will have 3 months to learn the course. You would have to give the final test within 3 months from the starting date.

Who will be the trainer?

This is an online internship program. The training content is provided by our educational partners which is a combination of text and video based tutorials for your training.

When will I get a certificate?

Every candidate who completes the training with 50% marks in the final test would be provided a certificate of completion from Isandatasystems pvt.ltd.

How can I clear my doubts

You can ask your doubts anytime by using get in touch option present on your training dashboard. Your questions will be answered by an internshipgate educational expert.
